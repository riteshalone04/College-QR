All students and staff must follow these rules while handling electronic equipment.

Lab Procedures and Experiments

In-Lab Workflow üìã

  1. Study the experiment aim, theory, and circuit diagram before entering the lab.
  2. Assemble the circuit neatly on the breadboard or trainer kit as per the diagram.
  3. Get the wiring checked by a staff member before switching on power supplies.
  4. Use CRO and DMM readings to verify that the circuit operates with expected waveforms.
  5. If the circuit fails, first confirm power rails, wiring continuity, and component orientation.
  6. After testing, switch off supplies, dismantle the setup, and record observations in the journal.
